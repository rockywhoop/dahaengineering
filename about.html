About Daha Engineering Consultancy

20+ years of excellence in engineering and project management

Our Story

Daha Engineering Consultancy was founded on the principle of cultivating meaningful relationships and creating value across all associations. We regard our customers, employees, partners, and investors as integral elements of our organizational fabric.

We provide implementation services and support to deliver comprehensive engineering and project management consultancy services. With expertise spanning engineering construction, energy, roads, railways, urban and rural development, telecommunications, utilities, and large-scale infrastructure projects, we have become a trusted partner in infrastructure development.

Our operational planning follows a structured, step-by-step execution process within the framework of an Accredited Quality Management System, with a clear focus on achieving total customer satisfaction.
