Renewable Energy & Solar Park Land Development

Complete surveying and development solutions for renewable energy projects

Service Overview

Daha Engineering delivers comprehensive surveying and site development solutions for renewable energy projects including solar parks, wind farms, and other clean energy installations. From initial site assessment and land demarcation to foundation design support and performance monitoring, we provide the technical expertise required for successful renewable energy project deployment.

Comprehensive Renewable Energy Services

Solar Park Land Demarcation

Precise boundary demarcation and area definition for solar park development, ensuring accurate land allocation for panel arrays and infrastructure.

Site Assessment & Topography

Comprehensive topographic surveys assessing terrain slope, drainage patterns, and surface conditions critical for solar panel placement and water management.

Soil Investigation & Analysis

Detailed soil testing and geotechnical analysis providing foundation design parameters for solar panel structures and inverter installations.

Foundation Design Support

Soil-structure interaction analysis and foundation recommendations for solar mounting systems, ensuring stability and safety over project lifespan.

Solar Panel Inspection & Monitoring

Drone-based thermal imaging and visual inspection for detecting defects, soiling, and performance issues in operational solar installations.

Wind Resource Assessment

Meteorological data collection and wind energy potential analysis for wind turbine site selection and capacity planning.

Drainage & Water Management

Site drainage design and stormwater management planning to prevent waterlogging and ensure operational safety of renewable installations.

Access Road & Infrastructure Planning

Survey and design support for internal roads, cable routes, and utility corridors required for project infrastructure development.

GIS Mapping & Spatial Analysis

Geographic analysis of land use, environmental constraints, and optimization of panel placement patterns for maximum energy generation.

GPS-Based Monitoring Network

Installation and maintenance of GPS stations for structural deformation monitoring and settlement tracking of renewable installations.

Performance Data Analysis

Collection and analysis of operational performance metrics, energy output tracking, and efficiency optimization recommendations.

Compliance & Environmental Assessment

Support for environmental impact assessment, regulatory compliance documentation, and sustainable development planning.

Technology & Equipment

  • Thermal Imaging Drones: High-resolution thermal cameras for panel defect detection
  • DGPS Systems: Precision positioning for infrastructure placement and monitoring
  • Soil Testing Equipment: Boring, sampling, and laboratory analysis capabilities
  • GIS Software: ArcGIS for spatial analysis and site optimization
  • 3D Modeling: Civil 3D for infrastructure design and visualization
  • Data Acquisition: Real-time monitoring sensors and data loggers
