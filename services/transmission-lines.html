Transmission Line Engineering & Surveying

Comprehensive services for transmission corridors and power infrastructure projects

Overview

Our Transmission Line Engineering & Surveying services provide end-to-end solutions for transmission corridors and power infrastructure projects. We combine cutting-edge technology with expert field analysis to deliver accurate, reliable data that supports project planning, design, and execution.

Key Services

Route Alignment Surveys

Precise route planning and alignment surveys for transmission corridors, ensuring optimal path selection.

DGPS & RTK Surveys

High-accuracy differential GPS and Real-Time Kinematic surveys for transmission corridor data.

Tower Spotting & Optimization

Strategic tower location identification and optimization for maximum efficiency.

Detailed Tower Location (DTL) Surveys

Comprehensive surveys providing exact tower positions and foundation data.

Profiling & Sag Tension Survey

Detailed elevation profiling and sag/tension data collection for design phase.

Check & Re-surveys

Quality verification surveys and re-surveys for accuracy assurance.

Crossing Surveys

Specialized surveys for crossings: roads, railways, rivers, canals, and other utilities.

Right of Way (ROW) & Land Acquisition Support

Comprehensive support for land acquisition, boundary surveys, and ROW documentation.

Stringing & As-Built Surveys

Construction phase surveys and final as-built documentation.

LiDAR Corridor Mapping

High-resolution LiDAR-based 3D corridor mapping for comprehensive terrain analysis.

GIS-Based Asset Mapping

Complete GIS mapping and documentation of all transmission assets for long-term management.

Underground Cable Route Surveys

Specialized surveys for underground and extra-high voltage (EHV) cable routes.
