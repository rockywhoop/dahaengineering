Highways, Railways & Expressways Engineering

Comprehensive surveying and engineering solutions for modern transportation infrastructure

Service Overview

Daha Engineering provides end-to-end surveying and engineering services for highways, expressways, and railway projects. Our expertise spans from initial route alignment and topographic surveys to detailed design support and construction monitoring. We leverage advanced DGPS technology and geospatial analysis to deliver accurate data for major infrastructure projects.

Comprehensive Highway & Railway Services

Route Alignment & Corridor Studies

Strategic planning and alignment studies for optimal route selection, considering terrain, environmental factors, and cost efficiency for highways and railways.

Topographic & Cadastral Surveys

Detailed topographic surveys providing accurate elevation data, ground conditions, and property boundaries essential for design and planning phases.

DGPS Positioning & Alignment

High-precision differential GPS technology for accurate horizontal and vertical alignment data, ensuring infrastructure meets design specifications.

Road Condition & Pavement Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of existing road conditions, structural integrity, and pavement quality for maintenance planning and rehabilitation projects.

Railway Corridor Surveys

Specialized surveying for railway projects including track alignment, gradient analysis, and overhead clearance verification for safe operations.

Bridge & Structure Design Support

Detailed site surveys for bridge locations, approach road design, and structural support systems with precise geotechnical data integration.

Utility Crossing Documentation

Identification and mapping of existing utilities (power, water, telecom) crossing highway and railway corridors to prevent conflicts and ensure safe construction.

Design Data & CAD Preparation

Conversion of survey data into CAD drawings and design-ready datasets, facilitating seamless integration with design engineering teams.

Cut & Fill Calculations

Accurate earthwork quantity calculations for cost estimation and construction planning, critical for project budgeting and timeline management.

Construction Stage Surveys

Regular monitoring and verification surveys during construction to ensure compliance with design specifications and quality standards.

Completion & As-Built Verification

Final surveys documenting as-built conditions, deviations from design, and comprehensive handover documentation for project closure.

GIS Mapping & Analysis

Geographic Information System analysis for corridors, land use mapping, and environmental assessment supporting project planning and stakeholder communication.

Technology & Equipment

  • DGPS Systems: Differential GPS with real-time correction for ±5cm accuracy
  • Total Stations: Electronic theodolites for precise angle and distance measurements
  • Level Instruments: Digital and optical levels for vertical alignment verification
  • GIS Software: ArcGIS for spatial analysis and corridor mapping
  • CAD Software: AutoCAD Civil 3D for design data preparation
  • Drone Technology: Aerial surveys and progress monitoring photography
