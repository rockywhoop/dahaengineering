Infrastructure Monitoring & Health Assessment

Advanced monitoring solutions for safety, performance, and predictive maintenance

Service Overview

Daha Engineering provides comprehensive infrastructure monitoring and health assessment services using advanced technologies including drone inspections, LiDAR scanning, thermal imaging, and IoT sensors. These services enable early detection of structural issues, optimize maintenance schedules, and ensure long-term safety and performance of critical infrastructure assets.

Comprehensive Infrastructure Monitoring Services

Drone-Based Inspections

Aerial visual inspection of bridges, towers, transmission lines, and buildings using high-resolution cameras capturing detailed condition data from safe distance.

Thermal Imaging & Analysis

Infrared thermal imaging for detecting temperature anomalies, energy losses, and hotspots indicating structural or operational issues.

LiDAR Scanning & 3D Modeling

High-precision laser scanning creating detailed 3D models for dimensional verification, deformation analysis, and structural health assessment.

Bridge Health Monitoring

Comprehensive bridge assessments including structural integrity analysis, crack detection, bearing condition evaluation, and scour evaluation.

Vibration Monitoring

Installation and analysis of vibration sensors on structures to detect dynamic behavior, resonance conditions, and potential safety concerns.

Structural Deformation Monitoring

GPS and tilt sensor networks tracking settlement, tilt, and displacement of structures over time enabling predictive intervention.

Tower & Transmission Line Inspection

Detailed inspection and assessment of transmission towers, power poles, and communication towers for structural soundness and maintenance needs.

Predictive Maintenance Analysis

Data-driven predictive models identifying maintenance requirements before failures occur, optimizing repair schedules and budgets.

Asset Deterioration Tracking

Long-term monitoring documenting condition trends and deterioration rates for evidence-based asset management decisions.

Monitoring Network Installation

Design and installation of comprehensive sensor networks for permanent or temporary structure health monitoring systems.

Cloud-Based Data Management

Cloud platforms for real-time data collection, storage, and analysis enabling remote monitoring and alert generation.

Condition Assessment Reports

Detailed condition reports with visual documentation, analysis results, and recommendations for maintenance and repairs.

Technology & Equipment

  • Drone Systems: Multi-rotor and fixed-wing drones with thermal and optical cameras
  • LiDAR Scanners: Terrestrial and airborne LiDAR for 3D modeling and dimensional accuracy
  • Thermal Cameras: High-resolution infrared imaging for anomaly detection
  • GPS/GNSS Systems: Real-time kinematic GPS for precise deformation monitoring
  • Vibration Sensors: Accelerometers and seismic sensors for dynamic monitoring
  • Cloud Platforms: IoT and cloud computing for real-time data management and analytics
